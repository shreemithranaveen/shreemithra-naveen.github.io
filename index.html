NLP Research · SemEval 2026
Can you tell if code was written by AI?
Situation

Banks, universities, and software teams couldn't tell AI-written code apart from human-written code , creating gaps in plagiarism detection, compliance, and code reviews that nobody had solved at scale.

Task

Build a reliable classifier that could make this distinction accurately , even on adversarial examples designed to fool it.

Action

Trained a CodeBERT classifier on 500,000+ code samples and layered in iterative multi-step prompt engineering to keep pushing accuracy. Submitted to SemEval 2026, one of the biggest NLP competitions globally.

Result

Achieved an F1 score of 0.78 on adversarial classification , a hard benchmark in a competition where most teams struggle to crack 0.70.

A job search agent that does the boring part for you
Situation

Applying to 5 jobs takes half a day , customizing resumes, writing cover letters, finding the right contact. The process is broken and doesn't scale for anyone doing a serious job search.

Task

Build an end-to-end automated system that handles the repetitive parts of job applications so you can focus on the conversations that actually matter.

Action

Agentic AI · Travel · 2026
🟢 Ongoing
An AI travel assistant that actually plans your trip
Situation

Travel planning is fragmented , you search flights on one site, hotels on another, and end up copy-pasting across tabs for hours. No tool brings it together intelligently.

Task

Build a multi-agent travel assistant where specialized agents collaborate , one handles flights, one handles hotels, one summarizes the full trip into a clean itinerary.

Action

Building with LangChain and Groq (free LLaMA 3 API) , three collaborative agents working together: a flight search agent, a hotel recommendation agent, and a trip summarizer agent that packages everything into a readable plan.

Result

Currently in development , designed to take a destination and budget as input and output a full trip plan in minutes, not hours.

IEEE Published · 2021–2023
A robot that actually cleans under your stuff
Situation

Every robot vacuum just drives around obstacles , bottles, toys, shoes , leaving those patches dirty. It was a fundamental limitation nobody had actually solved.

Task

Design an autonomous system that could detect, handle, and replace objects , not just avoid them.

Action
